Apple Crisp

This page will show the steps to make apple crisp

    Ingredients
  • 10 cups of apples
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Some other stuff idk

      • Steps

    2. put apples, flour, sugar, and cinnamon together in a pan
